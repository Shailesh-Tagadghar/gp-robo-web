Main Product

This robot can work as a Personal assistant at home and office, In the Industrial field like Hospitals, Hotels, Restaurants for (Take orders, deliver food and drinks just like a waiter) and for Repetitive tasks and boring tasks.

Robo is an Artificial Intelligence robot which is a 5 feet tall humanoid robot that can do similar activities like humans. We can interact with Robo through voice, it can understand our voice, talk back to us like a friend. Robo can see through the camera changing facial expression through display, move anywhere in autopilot mode. If Robo detects an unknown person or certain sounds when you're away, then it can send us a notification. This robot can do multiple tasks, Pick and place objects and Trackless Navigation (It does not need any track to move from one place to another).
